Return and Refund Policy

INTRODUCTION

At our e-commerce store, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We understand that sometimes products may not meet your expectations. This Return and Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for returning products and requesting refunds.

RETURN WINDOW

Standard Return Period: 30 days from the date of delivery
Electronics and Appliances: 15 days from the date of delivery
Personalized/Custom Items: Non-returnable unless defective
Software and Digital Products: Non-returnable once downloaded or activated
Perishable Goods: Non-returnable

ELIGIBILITY FOR RETURNS

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

1. Product must be unused and in the same condition as received
2. Product must be in original packaging with all tags, labels, and accessories
3. Proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt) must be provided
4. Return request must be initiated within the applicable return window
5. Product must not be on our non-returnable items list

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons:
- Intimate or sanitary goods (underwear, swimwear, earrings)
- Hazardous materials or flammable liquids
- Opened cosmetics, perfumes, or personal care products
- Perishable goods (food, flowers, plants)
- Gift cards and downloadable software
- Custom-made or personalized items (unless defective)
- Clearance or final sale items marked as non-returnable

HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN

Step 1: Log into your account and navigate to "My Orders"
Step 2: Select the order containing the item you wish to return
Step 3: Click "Return Item" and select the reason for return
Step 4: Choose refund method (original payment method or store credit)
Step 5: Print the prepaid return shipping label provided
Step 6: Pack the item securely with all original packaging and accessories
Step 7: Attach the return label and drop off at any authorized shipping location

RETURN SHIPPING

Free Return Shipping: Available for defective, damaged, or incorrect items
Standard Returns: $7.99 return shipping fee (deducted from refund)
International Returns: Customer responsible for return shipping costs
Exchange for Different Size/Color: Free return shipping provided

The return shipping fee will be automatically deducted from your refund amount for standard returns. If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact customer support for a free prepaid return label.

REFUND PROCESSING

Timeline:
- Return package received at warehouse: 1-2 business days
- Inspection and processing: 2-3 business days
- Refund issued: Within 5 business days after approval
- Refund appears in account: 5-10 business days (depending on payment provider)

Refund Methods:
- Original Payment Method: Refund credited to the card/account used for purchase
- Store Credit: Immediate store credit (no waiting period) with 10% bonus
- Bank Transfer: 5-7 business days processing time

REFUND AMOUNT

You will receive a full refund of the product price (excluding original shipping costs) if:
- Item is defective or damaged upon arrival
- Wrong item was shipped
- Return is initiated within 7 days of delivery

For returns after 7 days (but within 30 days), the following may apply:
- Full product refund minus return shipping fee ($7.99)
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable (unless error on our part)

Restocking Fee:
A 15% restocking fee may apply to:
- Opened electronics or appliances
- Items returned after 14 days
- Items missing original packaging or accessories

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

1. Contact customer support immediately (within 48 hours of delivery)
2. Provide photos of the damage/defect
3. We'll send a prepaid return label at no cost to you
4. Choose between full refund or free replacement
5. No restocking fees for damaged/defective items

EXCHANGES

We offer free exchanges for:
- Different size of the same item
- Different color of the same item
- Replacement of damaged/defective item

To request an exchange:
1. Follow the standard return process
2. Select "Exchange" as the reason
3. Specify the desired size/color
4. Free exchange shipping provided
5. New item ships immediately upon return receipt

Exchange Processing Time: 3-5 business days after return is received

LATE OR MISSING REFUNDS

If you haven't received your refund after the expected timeframe:

1. Check your bank account again
2. Contact your credit card company (may take time to process)
3. Contact your bank (processing time varies)
4. If you've done all of this and still haven't received your refund, contact us at refunds@ecommerce-example.com

PARTIAL REFUNDS

Partial refunds may be granted for:
- Items with obvious signs of use or wear
- Items not in original condition or packaging
- Items missing parts not due to our error
- Items returned more than 30 days after delivery

The partial refund amount is determined by the item's condition after inspection.

SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

Holiday Returns:
Items purchased between November 1 and December 25 can be returned until January 31 of the following year.

Warranty Claims:
Defective items outside the return window may qualify for manufacturer warranty service. Contact customer support for assistance with warranty claims.

Fraud Prevention:
We reserve the right to refuse returns from customers with excessive return rates or suspected fraudulent activity. Returns must be shipped from the same country where the order was delivered.

CANCELLATIONS

Orders can be cancelled free of charge within 1 hour of placement. After this time:
- Orders not yet shipped: Contact support for cancellation (usually successful)
- Orders already shipped: Must use standard return process after delivery

INTERNATIONAL RETURNS

International customers are responsible for:
- Return shipping costs
- Any customs duties or taxes incurred
- Ensuring proper customs documentation

Refunds for international orders exclude original international shipping fees and any customs charges paid.
