Frequently Asked Questions - E-commerce Customer Support

GENERAL QUESTIONS

Q: What types of products do you sell?
A: We offer a wide range of products including electronics, home appliances, fashion and apparel, sports equipment, books, toys, and home decor. Our catalog includes over 50,000 products from trusted brands and verified sellers.

Q: How can I create an account?
A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and fill in basic information like your name and shipping address. Account creation is free and takes less than 2 minutes.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. You can check availability and shipping costs for your country during checkout.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

ORDER RELATED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by logging into your account and viewing "My Orders" or by entering the tracking number on our tracking page. Updates are provided in real-time.

Q: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After this time, the order enters our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. If you need to cancel after 1 hour, you'll need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

Q: What if an item in my order is out of stock?
A: If an item becomes out of stock after you place your order, we'll notify you immediately via email. You can choose to wait for the item to be restocked (usually 5-7 business days), replace it with a similar item, or receive a full refund for that item.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery (available for an additional fee) takes 2-3 business days. International shipments take 10-15 business days depending on the destination country.

PRODUCT INFORMATION

Q: Are your products authentic?
A: Yes, we guarantee 100% authentic products. We work directly with authorized distributors and brands. All products come with manufacturer warranties and certificates of authenticity where applicable.

Q: Do products come with warranties?
A: Most electronics and appliances come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 6 months to 2 years. Warranty details are specified on each product page. We also offer extended warranty options for purchase during checkout.

Q: How can I find the right size for clothing?
A: Each clothing item has a detailed size chart on the product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to the size chart for the best fit. You can also check customer reviews for sizing feedback. If the size doesn't fit, we offer free size exchanges within 30 days.

Q: Can I see product reviews?
A: Yes, all product pages include verified customer reviews and ratings. You can filter reviews by rating, date, or verified purchase. We encourage customers to leave honest reviews to help others make informed decisions.

ACCOUNT & SECURITY

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link to create a new password. For security, the reset link expires after 24 hours.

Q: Is my personal information secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. Our privacy policy is available on our website.

Q: How do I update my shipping address?
A: Log into your account, go to "Account Settings," and select "Addresses." You can add, edit, or delete shipping addresses. Changes will apply to future orders but won't affect orders already placed.

PROMOTIONS & DISCOUNTS

Q: How do I apply a promo code?
A: During checkout, you'll see a field labeled "Promo Code" or "Discount Code." Enter your code and click "Apply." The discount will be reflected in your order total. Note that some promo codes cannot be combined with other offers.

Q: Do you have a loyalty program?
A: Yes! Our Rewards Program gives you 1 point for every dollar spent. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases. Members also get early access to sales, exclusive deals, and birthday rewards. Membership is free.

Q: When do sales happen?
A: We run major sales during Black Friday, Cyber Monday, holiday seasons, and seasonal clearances. Sign up for our newsletter to get notified about upcoming sales and exclusive offers.
