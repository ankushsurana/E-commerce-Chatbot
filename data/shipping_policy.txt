SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS

Geographic Restrictions:
- We ship to all 50 US states including Alaska and Hawaii
- PO Boxes accepted for standard shipping only
- APO/FPO addresses supported (processing time: 10-15 business days)
- Some remote areas may require additional delivery time

Product Restrictions:
The following items have special shipping requirements:
- Hazardous materials: Ground shipping only, additional 3-5 days
- Oversized items (furniture, large appliances): See "Oversized Shipping" section
- Refrigerated items: Express shipping only to maintain freshness
- Alcohol: Available in select states with age verification at delivery

DELIVERY

Delivery Hours: Monday-Saturday, 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM (varies by carrier and location)

ADDRESS ACCURACY

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate and complete:
- Include apartment/unit numbers
- Provide accurate phone number for delivery updates
- Use valid address (carriers cannot deliver to incorrect addresses)

We are not responsible for:
- Delays or non-delivery due to incorrect addresses
- Lost packages delivered to an address provided by customer
- Packages left as requested in delivery instructions

UNDELIVERABLE PACKAGES

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable:
- We'll contact you to verify the correct address
- Package can be reshipped (shipping charges apply)
- Refund available if you don't want reshipment
- Original shipping costs are non-refundable

LOST OR STOLEN PACKAGES

Carrier Lost:
If tracking shows package as lost during transit:
- Contact us within 30 days of order date
- We'll file a claim with the carrier
- Replacement shipped or full refund issued within 5-7 business days

Porch Piracy (Stolen After Delivery):
If tracking confirms delivery but you didn't receive the package:
- File a police report (required for claims over $200)
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery confirmation
- We'll investigate and work with the carrier
- Replacement or refund provided on case-by-case basis

Prevention Tips:
- Require signature at delivery
- Use a secure delivery location
- Install a security camera
- Consider package lockers or hold at carrier facility

DAMAGED SHIPMENTS

If you receive a damaged package:
1. Document damage with photos (box and product)
2. Do not discard packaging materials
3. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
4. We'll arrange free return shipping
5. Replacement sent immediately or full refund issued
